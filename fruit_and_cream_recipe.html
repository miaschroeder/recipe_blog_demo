Fruit & Cream Crepes

Description

These crepes are perfect if you love a refreshing, but indulgent dessert.

Ingredients

For the crepe batter

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt

For the cream

  • Cream Cheese
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Condensed Milk
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • Orange Zest

For the fruits

This part is really up to you :)

  • Strawberries
  • Mango
  • Peaches

Directions

Preparing the Crepes

  1. Bring your eggs, milk, and water to room temperature.
  2. Melt your butter, and then let it cool for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine your eggs, milk, and butter.
  4. Gradually incorporate your flour into the wet ingredients.
  5. Add your vanilla extract and salt.
  6. Refrigerate your batter for 30 minutes to over night
  7. Pan fry your crepes so that they are each thin and lighlty golden.

Preparing the Cream

  1. Bring your cream cheese to room temperature.
  2. Combine the cream chees, heavy whipping cream, and condensed milk.
  3. Whip until light and fluffy.
  4. Gradually incorporate your powdered sugar into the wet ingredients.
  5. Add your vanilla extract, salt, and orange zest.
  6. Refrigerate your batter for 30 minutes to over night
  7. Pan fry your crepes so that they are each thin and lighlty golden.
